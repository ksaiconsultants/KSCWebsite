About KS Consultants

KS Consultants was founded in Auckland, New Zealand, by Kent Somerville to help local businesses, schools, and professional services actually get value from AI — not just talk about it.

We started by supporting education leaders and small business owners who were being asked to “use AI” but weren’t given a safe, practical way to do it. Today we help teams adopt tools like Gemini and ChatGPT, build reusable prompt libraries, and automate the everyday admin that gets in the way of serving clients.

Our focus is simple: teach people, not just tools. If your team can use it tomorrow, we’ve done our job.

  • • Small business owners & professional services (accounting, legal, marketing)
  • • Mortgage & financial advisers
  • • Schools & education teams adopting AI responsibly
  • • Community organisations that need to do more with less
